Trip to ancient City of Kano

The ancient city of Kano is located in the Northern part of Nigeria. It has been
in existence since the pre-colonial era. Kano has 15 ancient city gates and several illegal
entrances commonly known as Haure.

The predominant tribe in the city are Hausas. The dominant religion in the city is Islam, followed by
Christianity and traditional religion. Their occupation is mainly commerce and agriculture, perhaps the
reason the state is referred to as the Centre of Commerce. Asides being popular for their groundnut production,
they also produce other crops like rice, maize, millet, potatoes, onions and sorghum.
