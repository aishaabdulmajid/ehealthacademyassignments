Required Ingredients

  • 3cups of parboiled rice
  • 4large size of tomatoes
  • 2large size peppper
  • 1 small bulb of onion
  • 2tbsp of vegetable oil
  • 3cubes of seasoning
  • 1/2 2tbsp of salt
  • 1/4 cup meat broth`

Procedure

Perboil your rice on medium heath. Blend your tomatoes, pepper and onions. Fry your blended pepper paste in the oil for about 15 minutes.
Add 2 large cups of water into the fried pepper. Add the seasoning and salt. You can also add your mmeat broth. Allow to boil for 10 minutes.
Pour in your washed parboiled rice. Cook on medium heat for 20minutes. Allow to steam and serve.

  • 1/2 bulb of small onion
  • 1/2 tbsp of oil
  • 1/2 seasoned cube of your choice

Procedure

Blend or grate your peppers, onion and tomatoes. Fry on low heat for 1mminute. Break your eggs into a clean bowl and add a pinch of salt
and the seasoning. Beat well and pour into the fried sauce. Stir gently on low heat for 2 minutes.
